What is Green Coding?
The term green coding means climate focused agenda to develop software climate friendly.
We host meetups and workshops to broaden the reach and knowledge about current climate related problems with software companies and ways that software is created. Through our community we hope to offer new ways of thinking and solutions and tackle the problem of climate crisis.
Buisness Rules
Businiesses often have climate feindliche routines that need to be transformed. From hosting, through in-person meetings and resource allocation.
Developers and Code
Coding is often repetitive and generated abundant amounts of unneeded data (Logs, Videos etc.)
Data Delivery
Data is often delivered uncached, over too long distances and in too big formats, which consumes unnecessary ressources.
